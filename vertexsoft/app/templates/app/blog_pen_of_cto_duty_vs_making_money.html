Duty vs Making Money ("Dehari Lagana")

 

In Pakistani Society a job is performed solely to make money. Since the purpose of job is to make money so during duty by which means money could be made is also kept in consideration by employees. In local language this process is also given a name and that is "Dehari Lagana" (Money Making).

For the purpose of "Dehari Lagana" (to make money), delegated authority by the employer are also misused. For example, if a person is doing job in a telecom company in Pakistan, for "Dehari Lagana" purpose, he will leak the sms of some one on request of his friends for good will or for money. If there is a police officer, he will harass other for "Dehari Lagana" on request of some of his buddy. If there is a bank manager, he will open a bogus account on the request of any of his buddy. List of "Dehair Lagana" is long and is present in every field.

The misuse of Authority for the purpose of "Dehari Lagana" has negative effect on the society, because someone is given extra favor in return of cash or good will and someone else is deprived of his due rights by keeping aside merit.

The thinking of "Dehari Lagana" is produced because a job is done not by keeping in mind that the job is to perform the duty, but by keeping in mind that job is just money making. Since the purpose is money making so by which means money could be produced on the job are also considered legitimate.

A job should only be done by focusing to deliver and to contribute to the society and to gain satisfaction through delivering. Of course, there are needs of the employee which are required to be fulfilled and money is required for it so compensation in the form of wage is also mandatory thing.

But if you think that your duty is not paying you much money to fulfill your needs, don't think of "Dehari Lagana" but think to do progress in your profession by moving to higher position or by switching through your commitment and dedication to the duty. Even if in worst case you have to switch your employer, it is better than sticking with one employer and start "Dehari Lagana".

Conclusion of the story is that job should be done by considering it duty. If you are delivering and employer is not compensating you then employer is not delivering his duty but it doesn't justify that you start "Dehari Lagana". You should try to move to a higher rank either by the same employer or to new one.
